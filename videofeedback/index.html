Video Feedback Lessons

Do you need feedback on your playing?


I teach a wide range of topics: preparing for a competition, wanting to learn more about Italian Renaissance & Baroque music as well as German, French, Spanish and English music, romantic, modernism, contemporary music and improvisation, being asked to play the bass continuo, but wanting to learn how to ornament and diminution, etc.

Although I am usually busy with teaching at two music universities and my own concert activities, I am open for the video feedback lessons to organ and harpsichord students at music college level and above, as well as professional organists and harpsichordists.
However, anyone wishing to take the entrance examination of Lugano University School of Music is always welcome.

Please contact me first using the contact form.

I have led my own students to numerous competition prizes & finals and CD awards.
